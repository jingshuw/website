**_Statistical Genetics · Computational Biology · General Statistical Methodology_**

<div style="margin-top: 0.5rem; border-bottom: 1px solid #d8d8d8; width: 50%;"></div>

We develop statistical methods and machine learning tools for modern biotechnologies and genetic data. Much of our work is motivated by problems in single-cell genomics and complex traits.

Current research directions include:

- <i class="fas fa-dna"></i> Single-cell multi-omics  
- <i class="fas fa-project-diagram"></i> Mendelian randomization and causal inference for heritable risk factors  
- <i class="fas fa-chart-line"></i> Large-scale inference and multiple testing, including p-value combination methods  
- <i class="fas fa-brain"></i> Latent-structure and factor models
